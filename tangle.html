Tangle Fidget

This is one of the first fidgets shipped to Wisconsin by Tangle Creations, specifically ordered by a toy collector and salesman, with the first sale going to Jack Rivers, who was enamored with the construction of the fidget itself.

You missed him in 1995. Now you have him. Now *she* has him, her son, forever and always. Too bad it was so messy.

The fidget allows for its user to constantly move the interlocking “macaronis” against one another, creating a satisfying sensation in the user's hands.
