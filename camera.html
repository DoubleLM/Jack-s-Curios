Classic Digital Camera

This digital camera was acquired from a garage sale being held by now-missing person Jackqui Holmes. Jackqui originally acquired this camera a year prior to its sale to Jack, but found that it simply never got used by her.

While the camera was originally intended to replace Jack's previous one, when it was announced that Jackqui had mysteriously vanished, Jack chose to put it in the museum to help raise awareness about her case.

The body is buried in Lake Kegonsa along the eastern shore.

If you have any information about the case, contact the Doveland Police Department or the Wisconsin State Patrol immediately.
