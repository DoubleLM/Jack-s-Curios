Beer Painting

This painting of Chilean beer brand Cerveza Cristal was acquired by a Canadian traveler during his time in Cuba, and later sold to Jack as part of an exchange.

The painting is a depiction of the canned version of the beer's logo. It also features some text presumably advertising exactly where the painting originated from.

Why would anyone travel with this, anyways? Makes for a good token, but it strikes me as strange.
