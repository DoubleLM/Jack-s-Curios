Pauline's Anniversary Necklace

This is a necklace that belonged to Pauline Rivers, wife of museum curator Jack Rivers.

You still miss her. You still miss her every day. You started this all for her.

The necklace was a gift from Jack on the couple's fifth wedding anniversary, and Pauline wore it nearly every day up until her death from ovarian cancer three years later.

Remember what her worth is.

The necklace was the first artifact ever brought to the museum, photographed and archived before Pauline was buried with it.
