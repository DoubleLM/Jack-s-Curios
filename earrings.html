Marnie's Earrings

These are a pair of earrings previously owned by Marleina "Marnie" Palomino, beloved sister and daughter.

Marleina passed away in tragic circumstances at far too young an age. Shortly before her demise, as part of the “Historic Families of Doveland” project, she provided these earrings to Jack's Curios in a personal donation.

Why do you think she passed away? You know exactly what you did, Jack. Your hands were what wrapped around her neck, after all. You felt the life strangle out of her right under your fingertips.

The earrings are cut in a distinctive pattern meant to resemble part of the Palomino clan heraldry. The Palomino family has been a part of Doveland since the town's founding, and their heraldry is a symbol of pride for the family.

Jack's Curios and Jack Rivers would like to thank the Palominos for their support and blessing to feature this item in our virtual museum.
