Hatsune's Doll

This doll, designed by longtime Doveland resident Hatsune Maeda, depicts a shrine maiden character from a classical Japanese children's story.

Maeda designed this as a line of plushies for the children of Doveland, WI, and has been selling them around the local area as “Fumo” plush dolls.

She's lucky Pauline was such good friends with her. You'd love to take her life, Jack.

The word "Fumo" comes from the Japanese onomatopoeia "モフモフ" (mofumofu), which means "soft to the touch", or "fluffy".

Actually, why don't you reunite old friends sometime? Do some good in this world and the next.
